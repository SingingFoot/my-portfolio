### What it is

Grammarly (or alternatives like LanguageTool) uses AI to improve grammar, tone, and readability. For technical writers, it ensures consistency and professional quality across all documents.

### How to use it

#### 1. Improve clarity

Run your documentation through Grammarly to:

- fix grammar  
- simplify sentences  
- improve tone  

---

#### 2. Maintain consistency

It helps standardize:

- terminology  
- tone of voice  
- sentence structure  

---

#### 3. Adjust tone

You can make your writing:

- more formal  
- more concise  
- more user-friendly  

---

### Best practice

Use it as a **final polishing tool**, not during initial drafting, to avoid interrupting your writing flow.
